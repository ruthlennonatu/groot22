Frequently Asked Questions

Feel free to ask us anything. Here at Groot's Global Green Initiative we are happy to face any queries you may have. We hope that the section below will answer your most critical questions. If you have any other query then please view the Contact US webpage for more information.

Do I qualify for a grant?

Wether you qualify for a grant or not depends on wether your income is below or above the income limit set out by the Global Green Initiative committee.

How much is the grant?

The amount of the grant varies depending on what details you put into the application form and on the decision made by the Global Green Initiative committee.

How to apply for the grant?

Applying for a grant is very easy, just visit our website and fill out the grant application form from your home PC.

How long do I have to wait for a grant decission?

All grants are first scanned by our machines and analysts before being presented to Global Green Initiative committee. The processing of a grant application can take up to 10 working days.

What documents do I need to get a grant?

In order to have the best chances of receiving a grant you must provide all the documentation requested by the Global Green Initiative committee. These documents include: Proof of Income - Your most recent payslip, Proof of ID - the photo of your drivers licence, Proof of address - Electricity bill. In addition to these 3 documents you also must provide your bank statements for the last 6 months. Once you get all these documents you will have a good chance of applying for a grant.

What if my application is rejected?

Sometimes we reject certain applications per decision of the Global Green Initiative committee. This occurs because the applicant dosen't meet the requirements set out in the form or failed to provide sufficient documentation. If this happens to you then you can always try to apply a 2nd time if you feel that you meet all the requirements.

How long does the grant last?

Currently the grant lasts as long as you don't violate the Terms&Conditionsof the grant that were set out by Global Green Initiative committee.

What if my financial situation changes?

If there's a change in your circumstances such as change of occupation, address or serious illness or any other change you should notify us of this change via Contact Us webpage or by calling our customer service at 086 000 0000 .

Does my health have an effect on the grant?

Yes, any change in your circumstances that directly affects you can have positive or negative impact on your current grant. It's important that you contact us so that we can keep your information updated at all times.

Do I have to fill out all details?

Fully completed forms get processed faster by the Global Green Initiative committee. The process lasts 7 working days if the form is fully completed and the proper documentation is provided.

How do I get in touch?

If you have any queries you can call us on 086 000 0000 or you can email us by using our Contact Us webpage.

Are there grant exceptions?

The Global Green Initiative committee can take exceptions into account if the applicant suffers from a serious illness or if there is a critical risk of life.
